### Touch Type

Mel magazine [found](https://melmagazine.com/en-us/story/youre-wasting-years-of-your-life-not-knowing-how-to-type-correctly) that an average worker types around 3,500 words a day including email, code, IMs, etc at a speed of 38‚Äì45 WPM.
